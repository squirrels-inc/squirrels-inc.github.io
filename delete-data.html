Data Deletion

App Data

Squirrel Scramble stores all game data (progress, high scores, settings) locally on your device. We do not store any data on external servers.

To delete all app data, you can either:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, or
  • Go to Settings > Apps > Squirrel Scramble > Storage > Clear Data

Third-Party Data

Our app uses third-party services that may store data independently. To manage or delete data held by these services, use the links below:

  • Google (AdMob, Play Games, Play Billing) — Manage your Google data
  • Google Ads personalization — Manage ad settings
